# Adidas Sales Analysis (Excel + Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Adidas retail sales performance to identify revenue drivers, profitability patterns, and strategic growth opportunities.

The goal is to transform raw transactional sales data into actionable business insights that support management decision-making.

---

## Business Questions

* Which regions generate the most revenue and profit?
* Which products should Adidas promote or reconsider?
* What sales channel performs best?
* How does performance change over time?

---

## Tools Used

* Excel → data cleaning and KPI creation
* Power BI → interactive dashboard & visualization

---

## Key KPIs

* Revenue
* Total Cost
* Profit
* Profit Margin

---

## Dashboard Pages

**Executive Overview**
Shows overall company performance and trends

**Product & Retail Analysis**
Identifies top and low performing products and retailers

**Strategic Insights & Recommendations**
Highlights best region, best channel, and improvement opportunities

---

## Key Insights

* Certain regions consistently outperform others in profitability
* Online sales channel generates the highest margin
* Some high-volume products contribute low profit
* Seasonal trends affect revenue performance

---

## Business Recommendations

* Invest more marketing budget in the West and Northeast regions
* Expand online sales channel
* Re-evaluate low-profit product categories
* Adjust inventory based on seasonal demand patterns
